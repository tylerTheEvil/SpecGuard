# --- PASS tier (clean, precise, verifiable) ---
REQ-01: The CVA6 processor shall execute the RV64I base instruction set, version 2.1.
REQ-02: The cache controller shall service a load-to-use access within 80 ns under nominal
        operating conditions (Vdd 1.2 V ± 5%).
REQ-06: The MMU shall translate virtual addresses using Sv39 paging, raising a page-fault
        exception when the PTE is invalid or the access permission check fails.
REQ-09: The FPGA fabric shall implement the CVA6 pipeline meeting a maximum operating
        frequency of 200 MHz at the worst-case process corner (SS, 0.95 V).
REQ-10: The interrupt controller shall acknowledge each level-sensitive interrupt within
        two clock cycles of the interrupt signal being asserted.

# --- WARN tier (seeded: vagueness, optionality, ambiguous adjective, comparative) ---
REQ-03: The system should be reasonably fast and user-friendly under typical load.
REQ-04: The interrupt handler may optionally clear some of the pending flags, etc.
REQ-07: The watchdog timer shall fire an adequate number of ticks before the deadline,
        depending on the specific operating mode in use at that time.
REQ-08: The FPGA configuration loader shall program the bitstream faster than the
        previous version of the loader, delivering better performance in all scenarios.

# --- FAIL tier (TBD placeholders — must be resolved before progression) ---
